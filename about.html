Meet Dakota McKnight-Todd

With 7 years of bookkeeping experience and a heart for helping small businesses thrive, I founded McKnight Todd Bookkeeping to give entrepreneurs the clarity and confidence they deserve in their finances.

My Story

I started my bookkeeping journey over seven years ago, working with a variety of businesses that all shared one thing in common: they wanted to understand their numbers but didn’t have the time or systems to make sense of them. I saw the relief that clear, reliable bookkeeping could bring—and I wanted to create that for more small business owners.

Today, I serve entrepreneurs and growing businesses across the U.S., helping them clean up their books, manage payroll, and stay tax-ready all year long. My mission is simple: make your financial life easier and empower you with clarity so you can focus on what matters most—your business and your family.

Beyond the Numbers

When I’m not reconciling accounts or preparing reports, you’ll find me spending time with my husband and kids, enjoying Oregon’s beautiful coast, or working on creative home projects. I believe balance and clarity go hand in hand and that’s what I strive to bring to every client relationship.
